Data Processing Agreement

This document outlines the responsibilities of TalentBridged Ltd to its customers regarding data protection and GDPR compliance.

TalentBridged as Data Processor, Definitions

TalentBridged is a Data Processor operating on behalf of its customers. Customers are individuals or organizations paying money to use the TalentBridged service. Free trial users of the TalentBridged Service are not Customers and should not send TalentBridged personal data.

TalentBridged's Customers are Data Controllers.

Definitions:

  • Personal data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable person.
  • Data Protection Laws: EU Directive 95/46/EC, as transposed into domestic legislation of each Member State, including the GDPR.
  • Services: TalentBridged's APIs and the professional services provided by TalentBridged.
  • Sub-processor: Any Data Processor engaged by TalentBridged.
  • Data Subject: The individual to whom Personal Data relates.

Processing of Personal Data

Use of the service implies that TalentBridged may process personal data on behalf of the Data Controller in accordance with Data Protection Laws. The Data Controller is responsible for ensuring instructions to TalentBridged comply with these laws.

Rights of Data Subjects

The Data Controller is responsible for obtaining all necessary consent from Data Subjects for TalentBridged to process personal data. TalentBridged will notify the Data Controller if it receives a Data Subject request and will not respond to such requests without prior written consent from the Data Controller.

Personnel

TalentBridged ensures that personnel engaged in processing personal data are informed of the confidential nature of the data, have received appropriate training, and have agreed to confidentiality obligations.

Sub-Processors

The Data Controller agrees that TalentBridged may engage third-party Sub-processors to provide the Services. These Sub-processors will only access personal data for the purposes of providing the services TalentBridged retained them for.

Security

TalentBridged agrees to implement and maintain appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards of personal data stored using the Services.

Security Breach Management and Notification

If TalentBridged becomes aware of unlawful access to the Data Controller's personal data, it will promptly notify the Data Controller, investigate the breach, and provide information about it. TalentBridged will follow its policies and procedures to mitigate the effects and minimize any damage from the breach.

Deletion of Customer Data

TalentBridged agrees to delete customer personal data in accordance with its procedures and Data Protection Laws. At a Customer's request, TalentBridged will provide a certification of deletion.

Governing Laws

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Ireland.

Legal Effect

This agreement comes into effect when a customer purchases a TalentBridged subscription and expires when the customer's subscription ceases.
